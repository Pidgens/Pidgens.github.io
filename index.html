Statistcal Analysis Crowdfunding
This is rudimentary analysis of the collective funding of the five desired countries. Notice Nepal and Philipines are by far the countries with the most funding.

Notice that Nepal doesn't have as much funding sources as the Philippines but has more funding than any other country in that list of desired countries. Nepal seems to be a country of interest seeing that they are able to get large sums of money from funding without that many sources.
